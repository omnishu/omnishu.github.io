5 smart things to know about repayment of home loan principal

2 Aug, 2015
Text: Centre for Investment Education and Learning (CIEL), contributions by Girija Gadre, Arti Bhargava and Labdhi Mehta

Here are five smart things that you need to know about the repayment of home loan principal.

Section 80C of the Income Tax Act

2 Aug, 2015
The principal repayment component in the home loan EMI is allowed as deduction under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.

Maximum tax deduction: Rs 1.5 lakh

2 Aug, 2015
The maximum tax deduction allowed under Section 80C is Rs 1.5 lakh, which includes investments in other instruments also.

Deduction allowed after construction is complete

2 Aug, 2015
This deduction is allowed only after the construction is complete and completion certificate is awarded to the buy

Registration charges also allowed to be claimed

2 Aug, 2015
Payment made towards stamp duty and registration charges are also allowed to be claimed under Section 80C in year in which paid.

  • Why providing ADHAAR number with your ITR is a better option?

    • 1. There has been a provision in the form for ITR which asks you to send the physical copy of ITR-V to CPC Bangalore by speed post or by any other ordinary postal service despite which sometimes it is observed the forms remain undelivered resulting in Rejection of ITR

    2. ADHAAR may be used to authenticate your e-filing making it easy for the department to acknowledge that you have duly filed your ITR and hence disposing off the process of sending the ITR-V to the Dept. making the process completely paperless. At present This Facility is available only to those Individuals who have income lesser than 5 Lacs per annum and do not have any refund during the current assessment year i.e. the year ending 31/03/2015

    3. Therefore it is advisable to provide your ADHAAR number as in near future this may benefit you as soon as the process of aligning the ADHAAR with ITR is accomplished.

Why Tax Planning

  • DEFINITION of 'Tax Planning':

    Logical analysis of a financial situation or plan from a tax perspective, to align financial goals with tax efficiency planning. The purpose of tax planning is to discover how to accomplish all of the other elements of a financial plan in the most tax-efficient manner possible.

When you take care of those ways in a legal way it is termed as Tax Planning.
